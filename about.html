Who We Are

Dynamic Equip SA (DESA) is a 100% black owned company which prides itself in quality and service. We specialize in sourcing and supplying high-grade mining equipment to the SADC Region, as well as local and international markets.

We deliver mining products and machinery that help our customers streamline their operations, improve process efficiency, and minimize downtime. Our goal is simple: reduce costs while maximizing performance through engineering excellence.
